üéØ Important Points to Remember

  • Sign Convention: Distances measured opposite to direction of incident light are negative
  • Convex lens: Positive focal length, converging lens
  • Concave lens: Negative focal length, diverging lens
  • Myopia (Short sight): Far point comes closer, corrected by concave lens
  • Hypermetropia (Long sight): Near point goes farther, corrected by convex lens
  • Presbyopia: Both near and far vision affected, corrected by bifocal lens
  • Dispersion: White light splits into VIBGYOR (7 colors)
  • Scattering: Blue light scatters more than red (why sky is blue)
  • Rainbow formation: Due to dispersion and total internal reflection in water droplets
  • Tyndall effect: Scattering of light by colloidal particles
