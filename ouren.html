9. Key Terms & Definitions

  • Ecosystem: A community of living organisms interacting with their environment
  • Biodiversity: Variety of life forms in an ecosystem
  • Food Chain: Linear sequence of energy transfer between organisms
  • Trophic Level: Position of organism in a food chain
  • Biomagnification: Increase in concentration of toxic substances up the food chain
  • Decomposers: Organisms that break down dead organic matter
  • Carbon Footprint: Total greenhouse gas emissions by human activities
  • Sustainable Development: Meeting present needs without compromising future generations
